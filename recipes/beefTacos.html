Beef Tacos

Beef tacos topped with salsa, onion, and coriander

Ingredients

  • Salsa
  • Beef shoulder
  • Coriander
  • Onion
  • Tacos
  • Taco seasoning

Steps

  1. Add beef shoulder, salsa, salt, pepper and taco seasoning to a slow cooker and cook on low for 8 hours
  2. Shred beef shoulder and place portions into tacos
  3. Garnish with salsa, onion and coriander
